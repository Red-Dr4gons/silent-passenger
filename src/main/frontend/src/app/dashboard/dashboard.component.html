Did you know, that consuming 1 litre of petrol produces 2.39kg of carbon dioxide.


An average person travels around 20.000km by car in a year. With an average fuel consumption of 7.0l/100km this produces more than 3 tons of carbon dioxide emissions. There are more than 1 billion people who drive cars on daily basis. If we again make a quick calculation this leads us to a result of 3 billion tons of carbon dioxide emission per year. But this is only produced by cars!

This terrifying number should encourage us to try and reduce the millage driven by a car when ever we can. So, if you have a package to deliver don't drive it there by yourself but rather find someone who is already making the trip and help save the environment for future generations. ;)
